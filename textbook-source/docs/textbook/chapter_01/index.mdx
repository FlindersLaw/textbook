This chapter walks you the process to creat an account and connect yourself to
the Docassemble server, which is where you will be writing your code.  This
chapter then takes you on a guided tour of what Docassemble looks like and
how you will use it to write code.

:::note
Not all students are required to work through [Connect to GitHub](00-20).
Please check with teaching staff before you attempt this part of the chapter.
:::
