Chapter 2. An Introduction to Coding with Docassemble

In this chapter we start teaching you how to code in Docassemble. You are welcome to read this chapter from beginning to end. However we recommend that you do so while at the same time working through the Chapter 2 Coding Exercises

Our experience has been that students that read through this chapter without practising as they go face more difficulties understanding the material than students who attempt the coding exercises as they read through this chapter.
